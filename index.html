Who am I?






What am I on this planet to do?


I promise to live!


"Being in the moment, awake and alive and able to enjoy things now. Not worrying about the past or postponing happiness to a distant future." - Dr. Joos Meyer






What are my gifts?

  • To bring leadership

  • To be selfless

  • To create

  • To bring positivity

  • To fix


To bring leadership


  • Program Manager

  • As a strategic project-management professional whose job is to help oversee and coordinate the various projects. I hope that I can be lead by example.

  • Community Leader

    • As an individual with a strong sense of helping others. I want to work together with everyone in the community, to mobilize and guide others. I want to make a brighter future for everyone.


To be selfless


  • Volunteer

  • As a volunteer, I will freely give my time and labour for community service. I will be focus on helping the homeless and healthcare.

  • Nurse

  • As a professional caregiver for patients. I will help manage physical needs, prevent illness, and treat health conditions of those that are in need.


To create


  • Software Engineer

  • As a developer, I want develop systems and software for businesses. Also, I want to create awesome projects to inspire others.

  • Artist

  • As an individual that loves art. I want to practices any of the various creative arts, such as a sculpting, painting, or filmmaker.


To bring positivity


  • Influencer

  • I want to be an influencer. My main focus is to help others learn about financial literacy, real estate and home renovations.

  • Dance Instructor

  • I love to dance. I enjoyed teaching others. I feel free when I dance.


To fix


  • Landscape Architect

  • I love to plan, design, managem, and nurture any the built and natural environments. One day I want to create a cool green space that allows for sustainable urban living.

  • DIYer

  • I just love breaking, learning and creating cool pieces. Do It Yourself is the way to go!






What will I do to achieve?


I will devote myself to explore life has to offer! I want to make sure that I will continuous to learn new knowledge everyday. I will be selfless, generous, a creative, a fixer, a leader and bringing positivity to everyone. I hope to gain successful mentors that can help me reach my goals. This is a promise! A planet responsibility that I want to fulfill.
